Why this kick-off matters

Legal and compliance teams are being asked to sign off on AI systems and AI agents that operate in high-stakes environments. Leaderboards and generic benchmarks are not enough. What they need are custom evaluations that reflect real tasks, risk tolerances, and institutional duties.

Looking toward 2026

This December 3 session will be a short, high-signal kick-off: aligning on goals, shared language, and priority use cases. The work then continues in 2026 through a series of focused research sprints, working groups, and convenings on evaluation practices for legal AI and agentic systems.

The Dec 3 Brain Trust

This initial group will help set the direction for the 2026 agenda, bringing perspectives from legal practice, agent evaluation platforms, consumer protection, and computational law.
